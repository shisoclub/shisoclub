Our Thoughts
1. To believe you need to be old and seasoned to make change is erraneous.
2. To believe change cannot exist without a community of change-makers is naive.
3. To believe change only exists in the form of entrepreneurship is narrow.
4. To believe community can only be forged out of legacy is strange.
5. To believe change is only born out of prestige is foolish.
6. To believe in the possibility of change is honorable.
